Flavors We Offer

What Flavor Will You Love The Most?

ORYZA WHIP BREW

If you are the type of person who does not have a sweet tooth. Worry no more, as the whip brew is there to enjoy the taste of our rice coffee. The whip brew is composed of purely rice coffee with whip cream at the top to balance the bitterness and sweetness of the coffee.

ORYZA MILKY STRAWBERRY

If you like a fruity taste, the milky strawberry is perfect for you. It gives off the fruity,sweet vibe of rice coffee. From the name itsef, Milky Strawberry balances the sweetness, fruitlines, and taste of signature rice coffee.

ORYZA CHOCO COFFEE

Choco Coffee is one of the signature iced coffees of Oryza Cup, as it gives a balanced of taste of chocolate and rice coffee. This is perfect for you if you have a sweet tooth.
